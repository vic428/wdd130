Overview

Purpose

Melisstandard is a small catering business that commenced its operations over 4 years ago. This website would provide an online presence for this catering business thus making it accessible to a wider audience and potential clients. It would also showcase all of its catering portfolio’s using high quality photos of dishes, event setups, customer reviews and testimonials from satisfied clients which could help potential customers see the quality of services rendered by the brand and to build trust and credibility.

With this platform we’d display menu options and pricing so potential customers understand what we’re offering and their corresponding prices, making it easier for them to decide if our services match their needs and budget.To encourage inquires and bookings from both interested clients,we’ll have contact information included so the brand can be contacted promptly. We’re also committed to increasing visibility across all platforms by integrating links to the brands social media pages allowing visitors follow the business on these platforms.

Audience

Catering services can be relevant to various individuals and groups. Here are some of the primary audiences for a catering website:
1.Individuals Planning Events: This includes individual’s planning weddings, birthday parties, anniversaries and other private and public events. They often seek catering services to provide food and beverages for their special occasions.
2.Individuals and Families that have minimal time to prepare their own meals could visit the website to learn of meal cooking and delivery plans to their houses.
3.Corporate Clients: Businesses and organizations often require catering for meetings, conferences, seminars and corporate events.

4.Professional Event Managers: Professional event planners and co-ordinators may visit catering websites to find reliable catering partners for their clients’ events. Building relationships with event planners can lead to recurring business.
5.Venue Owners and Managers: Owners and managers of event venues such as banquet halls, conference centres and hotels, may refer clients to catering services. They might visit catering websites to evaluate potential catering partners.
6.Foodies and Food Enthusiast: Generally, some people have a passion for food and culinary experiences. These might visit catering websites to explore menus, discover new dishes, or follow a catering company’s culinary journey.
7.Local Community: Members of the local community, may visit the site to learn more about catering services offered by the brand. This can include neighbours, friends and local businesses.
8.Vendors and Suppliers: Vendors and suppliers who would like to explore potential partnership options with the brand, may visit the website as well.
